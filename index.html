Frequently Asked Questions

Is the University Government Recognized?
Yes, TNU is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956. We hold all necessary statutory approvals for our programs.
What equipment is provided for B.Des students?
Students have access to high-performance computer labs, industry-standard design software, digital drawing tablets, professional DSLR cameras, and AI-powered production tools.
Are transport and hostel facilities available?
Yes, TNU provides secure and comfortable separate hostels for boys and girls on campus. Transport facilities are also available from various parts of Kolkata.
What are the career prospects after this course?
Graduates can expect dynamic careers as UI/UX Designers, Motion Graphic Designers, Brand Identity Experts, and Generative AI Design Specialists in highly reputed IT and media firms.
